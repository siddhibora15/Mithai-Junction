Our Sweet Journey

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Master Halwai Rajesh Kumar, Mithai Junction began as a small sweet shop with a big dream - to share the authentic flavors of traditional Indian sweets with the world.

What started as a family recipe book has now grown into a beloved brand that serves thousands of customers daily, while maintaining the same commitment to quality and authenticity that our founder instilled from day one.

Today, we continue to honor traditional methods while embracing modern hygiene standards and customer service excellence.
